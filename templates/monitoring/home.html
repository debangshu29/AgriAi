How To Use Without A Drone

Prototype with public imagery

  1. Train the classifier on PlantVillage or PlantDoc leaf datasets.
  2. Train the segmenter on aerial field datasets such as Agriculture-Vision.
  3. Upload public drone shots or satellite-like field images into this dashboard.
  4. Later swap the upload source for a real drone feed or edge device camera.
